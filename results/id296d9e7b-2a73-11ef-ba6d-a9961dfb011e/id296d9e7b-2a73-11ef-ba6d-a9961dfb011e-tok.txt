Upon receiving a report from a board of inquiry the President may direct the Attorney General to petition any district court of the United States having jurisdiction of the parties to enjoin such strike or lock - out or the continuing thereof , and if the court finds that such threatened or actual strike or lock - out affects an entire industry or a substantial part thereof engaged in trade , commerce , transportation , transmission , or communication among the several States or with foreign nations , or engaged in the production of goods for commerce ; and if permitted to occur or to continue , will imperil the national health or safety , it shall have jurisdiction to enjoin any such strike or lockout , or the continuing thereof , and to make such other orders as may be appropriate .
