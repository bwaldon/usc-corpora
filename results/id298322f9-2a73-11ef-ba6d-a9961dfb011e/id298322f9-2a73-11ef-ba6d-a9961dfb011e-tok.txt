Notwithstanding the provisions of subsection ( a ) which relieve an employer from liability and punishment with respect to any activity , the employer shall not be so relieved if such activity is compensable by either an express provision of a written or nonwritten contract in effect , at the time of such activity , between such employee , his agent , or collective - bargaining representative and his employer ; or a custom or practice in effect , at the time of such activity , at the establishment or other place where such employee is employed , covering such activity , not inconsistent with a written or nonwritten contract , in effect at the time of such activity , between such employee , his agent , or collective - bargaining representative and his employer .
