Any employer may employ any employee for a period or periods of not more than 10 hours in the aggregate in any workweek in excess of the maximum workweek specified in subsection ( a ) without paying the compensation for overtime employment prescribed in such subsection , if during such period or periods the employee is receiving remedial education that is provided to employees who lack a high school diploma or educational attainment at the eighth grade level ; designed to provide reading and other basic skills at an eighth grade level or below ; and does not include job specific training .
