When used in this chapter The term “ industry affecting commerce ” means any industry or activity in commerce or in which a labor dispute would burden or obstruct commerce or tend to burden or obstruct commerce or the free flow of commerce . The term “ strike ” includes any strike or other concerted stoppage of work by employees ( including a stoppage by reason of the expiration of a collective - bargaining agreement ) and any concerted slowdown or other concerted interruption of operations by employees . The terms “ commerce ” , “ labor disputes ” , “ employer ” , “ employee ” , “ labor organization ” , “ representative ” , “ person ” , and “ supervisor ” shall have the same meaning as when used in subchapter II of this chapter .
